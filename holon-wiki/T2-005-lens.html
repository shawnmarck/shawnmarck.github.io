T2-005 — Lens WHAT

The vocabulary subset selector.

Divergence: GUIDE vs Rust

The GUIDE (lines 1260–1266) describes a flat MarketLens with 6 variants: :momentum, :structure, :volume, :narrative, :regime, :generalist. The actual Rust has replaced this with 11 variants organized into 3 named schools (Dow, Pring, Wyckoff). This is a complete restructuring, not a minor update.

The GUIDE also describes exit lenses (:volatility, :structure, :timing, :generalist) that map to wat vocab modules. The Rust has two position lenses (Core, Full) that map to completely different Rust-only vocab modules (exit regime, exit time, phase).

⚠️ No Wyckoff Generalist

There is no WyckoffGeneralist variant. The Wyckoff school has 3 specialist lenses but no generalist. This contrasts with Dow (which has DowGeneralist) and Pring (which has PringGeneralist).
