# Grace vs Violence

> **TL;DR:** The system's accountability labels. Grace = the thought produced value (profit). Violence = the thought destroyed value (loss). They're not booleans — they carry weight. A strong Grace teaches harder than a marginal one.

## The Labels

The enterprise measures everything through two outcomes:

- **Grace** — the trade produced residue. Capital deployed, position ridden, principal recovered, permanent gain accumulated. The thought was true.
- **Violence** — the trade destroyed value. Capital deployed, stop fired, loss bounded by reservation. The thought was false.

These are the accountability pair in the learning system. [[Market Observers]] learn from Up/Down. [[Brokers]] learn from Grace/Violence. The labels carry **weight** — the magnitude of the outcome determines how much the reckoner learns. A large Grace teaches harder than a marginal one.

## The Semantic Depth

The naming is deliberate. These aren't "win" and "loss" — sterile accounting terms. **Grace** implies something was given freely, a gift from the market. **Violence** implies something was taken by force, a cost imposed by reality. The thought either produced harmony with reality or clashed with it.

This framing carries architectural weight:

- "More Grace, more capital" — the [[The Treasury]] funds proportionally to edge
- "More Violence, less capital" — brokers that produce Violence lose allocation
- "The Grace/Violence ratio IS the answer to 'do we trust this team of observers?'" — from the GUIDE

## The 2×2 Grid

Side × Direction forms a truth table — a **theorem** when the system is coherent:

| | Up (price rose) | Down (price fell) |
|---|---|---|
| **Buy** | Grace ✓ | Violence ✗ |
| **Sell** | Violence ✗ | Grace ✓ |

This is not a definition. It's a derived property that holds when the system is functioning correctly. **Incoherence** — the system acting against its own prediction — is where the machine learns the most. That's why outcomes are measured independently rather than assumed from the prediction.

## Grace in Practice

When a trade settles with Grace:
1. The trailing stop fires after the price moved favorably
2. Exit amount > principal → residue is permanent gain
3. The principal returns to available capital for redeployment
4. The residue compounds — it is never withdrawn by the enterprise
5. The broker routes Grace to its observers, strengthening the signals that predicted correctly

## Violence as Information

Violence isn't failure — it's **teaching**. When a trade settles with Violence:
1. The safety stop fires (or the trailing stop fires below principal)
2. Loss is bounded by the reservation — capital at risk was known in advance
3. The broker routes Violence to its observers, weakening the signals that predicted incorrectly
4. The [[Conviction-Accuracy Curve]] incorporates this data point, refining future predictions

The system needs both labels to learn. A reckoner that only sees Grace has no discriminant — no direction to separate. Violence provides the contrast that gives the discriminant its shape.

## Proposal 008: The Accumulation Model

The accumulation model formalizes Grace as permanent growth: deploy capital, recover principal, keep the residue. The residue IS the growth. It compounds. Both sides of any asset pair grow through this mechanism — the pair doesn't matter, the direction doesn't matter.
