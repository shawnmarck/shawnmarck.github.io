How Proposals Work#

The trading lab evolves through formal proposals. Each describes a problem discovered through simulation, wards, or design review. Proposals are reviewed by the panel (Beckman, Hickey, and domain-specific reviewers). A resolution synthesizes reviews into a final decision.

The proposal system is distinct from the wat language proposals — these govern the trading lab architecture (userland), not the language itself.
