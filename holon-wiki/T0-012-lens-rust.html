Lens Wiring (lens.rs)

Shared Module (all market lenses)

Every MarketLens variant includes shared::time (encode_time_facts). This is encoded once before the match, not repeated per arm.

Market Lens → Module Map

All modules are in vocab::market::* unless noted otherwise.
